The Dancer

(Nehtor, God of Healing)

The Dancer constellation is made up of eight stars in a seemingly random arrangement. Many astronomers see the picture of a slender Dancer within their location. The constellation is more often connected with Nehtor, the God of Healing, for which the constellation was named.
